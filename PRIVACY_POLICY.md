# Privacy Policy

**Cleared2Hire**
Last updated: April 24, 2026

## Overview

Cleared2Hire ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Cleared2Hire mobile application (the "App"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Email address and password (used for authentication via Supabase Auth)
- User type (candidate or employer)

**Candidate Profile Information** (candidates only)
- Full name, location
- Security clearance level and status
- Skills, certifications, years of experience, seniority level
- Availability status and target roles

**Employer Information** (employers only)
- Company/organization association
- Job postings and hiring pipeline activity

**Usage Data**
- Match interactions (accept, decline, pass, interest signals, interview requests)
- App navigation and session data

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, photos, or biometric data.

## How We Use Your Information

- To match cleared candidates with security-cleared job opportunities
- To facilitate communication between candidates and employers through the pipeline
- To verify eligibility and manage access (invitation-only platform)
- To improve app functionality and fix bugs

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Candidate profiles are only revealed to employers when a mutual match is established. We use Supabase (supabase.com) as our backend database and authentication provider. Data is stored on servers in the United States.

## Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth. Access to candidate profiles is governed by row-level security policies — employers can only view profiles of candidates matched to their specific job postings.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

Cleared2Hire is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via the App or email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
